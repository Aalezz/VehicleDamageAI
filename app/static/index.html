Process

Professional inspection in three steps

The same assessment flow used by body shops and insurance adjusters — compressed into under a minute.

STEP 01

Identify the vehicle

Select the make, model and year. Repair pricing is calibrated to that specific vehicle — a bumper on a 2024 BMW is not priced like one on a 2010 Corolla.

STEP 02

Capture the evidence

Upload one photo of the damage, or walk around the vehicle for a complete pre-purchase inspection. Standard phone photos are sufficient.

    FAQ

    Common questions

    How accurate is the AI?
    The damage detector reaches 82% mAP@0.5 on validation data and the severity classifier reaches ~89% accuracy. Results are strongest on clear, well-lit exterior photos. Every report includes confidence scores per finding, and heatmaps show what the model looked at — so you can judge each result yourself.
    Can it tell if a car was repainted or previously repaired?
    Not yet. Detecting resprayed panels and hidden past repairs requires a dedicated model that is on our roadmap. Today Suraya Car assesses visible body damage. For accident history, always combine the report with a VIN history check.
    Are the repair costs guaranteed?
    No — they are calibrated estimates based on US average body-shop pricing, adjusted for your vehicle's brand tier and age. Actual quotes vary by region and shop. Use the range as a negotiation baseline, not a binding quote.
    What happens to my photos?
    Photos are processed in memory for the analysis and are not shared with third parties. Assessment results (not the photos) are stored in your account history so you can review past reports.
    Do you offer an API?
    Yes. Pro and Business plans include REST API access with per-user API keys. Full interactive documentation is available at /docs once you have a key.
